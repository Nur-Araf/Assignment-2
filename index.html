Daily Task

This is some exercises to be fit in your daily life.Maintain them to lead a disease free life

Push Ups

Push-ups work your chest, shoulders, triceps, and core, making them an excellent upper body exercise. They also enhance core stability and can be modified to match your fitness level.

Burpees

Burpees combine strength training and cardio, targeting your legs, core, and upper body. They are highly effective for burning calories and building endurance.

Squats

Squats target your glutes, quads, hamstrings, and calves, helping to build lower body strength. They also improve balance and can be performed with or without weights.

Planks

Planks engage your entire core, including your abs, back, and shoulders, promoting stability and strength. Holding a plank also enhances endurance and posture.
